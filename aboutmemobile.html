I am an illustator and painter based in Queens, New York. As a youth, I always dreamed of becoming an artist. I was inspired by my older cousin, who had a special talent in drawing Dragon Ball Z characters. Throughout my childhood, he gave me great advice and taught me techniques to becoming a better artist. Once I entered High School, I was put in AP art courses, which challenged me and pushed me to become the artist I am today. After graduating High School, I then went on to get my AS degree in Fine Arts from LaGuardia Community College. There, I learned amazing drawing and painting techniques that really took my skills to the next level. Now, I am in Queens College majoring in Graphic Design with the hopes of recieving a bachelors degree and implementing some digital work into my art using adobe softwares such as photoshop, illustrator, premier, InDesign, etc.
