Who is Filmer Kev?

Filmer Kev is a New York artist who focuses his work in video and photography. Focusing on two medias is what currently takes his interest and working as a freelancer in NYC definitely drives his art direction further. Coming up from Elmhurst, Queens, life was full of ever changing culture and media, so naturally adapting and moving with it guided him to his art vision currently.
Current works have been showcased in some locations; Ink on Skin Documentary was showcased at an art exhibit in Corona, Queens. Some prints have been shown at local small viewings between friends and family. From these small exhibits and youtube videos tp future goals of having his films seen worldwide and his artistic vision empowering and inspiring many others.
